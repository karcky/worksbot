About Us

Lee Chong Badminton Academy was started in the year 2011 by popular badminton players Ajay Kanwar and Manjusha Kanwar. The couple established the academy in East Delhi. It is the only academy in North India that is affiliated to Pullela Gopichand Academy, Hyderabad. Ajay Kanwar, who was one of the founders of the academy, participated in the Thomas Cup, International Championships and World Badminton Championships. His wife Manjusha Kanwar also has many titles under her belt. Establishment Year: 2011 Present Address: Yamuna Sports Complex, Surajmal Vihar, East Delhi Days and Timings: Tuesday to Sunday, 3:00 PM to 6:30 PM Eligibility: The minimum age required to join this academy is 6 years Registration and Fees: There is no registration fee to join this academy. However, the monthly fee for members is Rs. 2,500, while the monthly fee for non-members is Rs. 3,125. How to Apply: Interested aspirants can directly visit the academy during its working hours to enrol their names.
